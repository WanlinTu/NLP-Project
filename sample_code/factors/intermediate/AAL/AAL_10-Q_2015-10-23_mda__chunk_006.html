Regional Operating Expenses

Total regional expenses decreased $383 million, or 7.8%, in the first nine months of 2015 to $4.5 billion from $4.9 billion in the 2014 period. The period-over-period decrease was primarily due to a $603 million, or 38.3%, decrease in fuel costs, offset in part by a $220 million, or 6.6%, increase in other regional operating expenses. The average price per gallon of fuel decreased 40.9% to $1.81 in the first nine months of 2015 from $3.06 in the 2014 period. The increase in other regional operating expenses was principally due to increased flying under capacity purchase agreements.
