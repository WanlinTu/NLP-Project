Income Taxes
In 2021, we recorded an income tax benefit of $555 million at an effective rate of approximately 22%, which was substantially non-cash. At December 31, 2021, we had approximately $17.2 billion of gross federal NOLs and $3.0 billion of other carryforwards available to reduce future federal taxable income, of which $6.9 billion will expire beginning in 2024 if unused and $13.3 billion can be carried forward indefinitely.
