The description of the activities below has been provided to us by Warburg Pincus LLC (“WP”), affiliates of which: (i) beneficially own more than 10% of our outstanding common stock and are members of our board of directors and (ii) beneficially own more than 10% of the equity interests of, and have the right to designate members of the board of directors of, Santander Asset Management Investment Holdings Limited (“SAMIH”).  SAMIH may therefore be deemed to be under common “control” with us.  However, this statement is not meant to be an admission that common control exists.
